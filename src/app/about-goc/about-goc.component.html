About GOC

GOC is a leading exporter of seafood products to Thailand. Recently, we have expanded our market to Korea, Japan, Singapore, Malaysia and Hong Kong. We offer a wide selection of premium seafood including fresh water prawns, crabs, pomfrets and various fishes.

With over 15 years of experiences in the industry, we source, process and deliver with strict measurements to meet the international requirements for exporting seafood. We prioritize freshness and hygiene and guarantee the quality of our products. With these operating factors, we have already established a good image as reliable supplier in Thai market.

We keep our reputation by consistently offering a variety of higher quality seafood at reasonable prices. Our products are tailored to satisfy customers' needs and are available for shipment around the world. Please feel free to contact us if you would like to receive more information about how Golden Orient can assist you with your requirement.
